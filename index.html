PURPOSE

He purpose of making Soto Kudus is to serve a delicious and nutritious dish with an authentic taste from Kudus, Central Java. Besides being a daily meal, Soto Kudus is also made to preserve traditional cuisine, introduce regional culture, and be a special dish for family gatherings or celebrations..

Ingredients

1 kg Cut Chicken
200 g Long Bean Sprouts
16 Cloves Shallots
10 Cloves Garlic
2 Stalks Green Onions
5 Sprigs Celery Leaves
5 Bay Leaves
8 Kaffir Lime Leaves
2 Inches Ginger
6 Roasted Candlenuts
2 Stalks Lemongras
1 tbsp Palm Sugar
1 tbsp Salt
Vermicelli
½ tbsp Ground Coriander
1 tsp Ground Cumin
1 tsp Ground Pepper
1 tsp Mushroom Stock Powder
2.5 Liters Water

Tools

Pan
Stove
Chopper
Knife
Chopping Board
Ladle
Bowl

Tutorial

Follow these steps to prepare Soto Kudus:

    Prepare the Spice Paste

    1. Blend shallots, garlic, toasted candlenuts, and ginger with a little water until smooth.

    2. Sauté the spice paste over medium heat until fragrant and slightly changes color.

    3. Add lemongrass, bay leaves, ground coriander, ground cumin, and ground white pepper. Stir well over low heat.

    Boil the Chicken

    1. Bring 2.5 liters of water to a boil in a pot.

    2. Add the cleaned chicken.

    3. Add bay leaves, ginger, and lemongrass.

    4. Simmer until the water reaches a rolling boil.

    Combine and Season

    1. Add the sautéed spice paste to the boiling water.

    2. Stir in palm sugar, salt, and mushroom stock powder.

    3. Mix well and let the chicken cook until fully tender. Adjust the seasoning to taste.

    4. Once the chicken is cooked, shred it into bite-sized pieces.

    Prepare the Bowl and Arrange the Ingredients

    1. Take a serving bowl and arrange the cooked rice noodles, bean sprouts, and shredded chicken.

    2. Sprinkle chopped green onions, celery, and fried shallots on top.

    Pour the Broth and Serve

    1. Pour the hot broth over the ingredients in the bowl.

    2. Serve with lime wedges, chili sauce, and crackers.
